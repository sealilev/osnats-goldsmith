Accessibility Statement

Osnat Levy believes every person deserves equal access to information and services online. This website strives to meet the requirements of Israeli Standard 5568 (WCAG 2.1, Level AA) and the Israeli Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Regulations (2013).

Accessibility Features

  • Alternative text descriptions for all images
  • Full keyboard navigation support
  • Skip-to-content link on every page
  • Proper heading hierarchy for screen readers
  • Color contrast meeting AA requirements
  • Support for both RTL (Hebrew) and LTR (English) layouts
